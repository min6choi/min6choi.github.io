2. What does the size of nodes mean?
  So, are all the words connected with each other eventually? Yes, right. However, it may be different because of the number of times ‚Äòlove‚Äô and ‚Äòcats‚Äô are connected, and the number of times ‚Äòlove‚Äô and ‚ÄòI‚Äô are connected. Therefore, we used a specific process which is ‚Äòtextrank‚Äô to calculate the importance and degree of connection for each word using the number of times. The word which appears the most will be the most important and the two words will be more connected if the frequency of occurrence of two specific words together is high. When managing words, the frequency of their appearance is important. Therefore, the more it appears, the larger the node size, and vice versa. Also, the 100 words selected in the graph were also selected from 100 words with high importance according to their frequency of appearance.
